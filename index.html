Horse Vet

Who am I?

My name is Dr. Pedro "Memo" Morales. I am a dedicated Equine Veterinarian with over 30 years in animal care. From racehorses to range stock I provide as a Horse Veterinarian medical assistance to horses, donkeys, mini horses, cows and more.

As a practicing veterinarian, I've set broken bones, diagnose diseases and even care for quarantine sick animals for exporting purposes. I've instructed the owners on better cleaning, exercising, feeding, nutritional or grooming practices.

Routine preventative health care while concentrating on equine athleters for sports such as Western, Sorting, Cutting, Team Penning, Jumping, Dressage and Toros Coleados (a.k.a Coleo, a traditional equine sport in Venezuela)

Sports Medicine

Wellness exams, check-ups to diagnose sport injuries or minor ailments, minor joint injuries or inflammations, lameness diagnose and treatment.

Surgery & Radiology

I've worked at a Racetrack tending strained muscles, rabdomiolisis, injured feet.
I've pre-race examined to determine if they are healthy enough to compete
